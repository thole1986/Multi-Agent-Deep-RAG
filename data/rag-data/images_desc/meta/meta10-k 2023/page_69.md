**Summary of Financial Data:**

**Daily Active Users (DAUs):**
*   Worldwide DAUs increased 6% to 2.11 billion (average December 2023) from 2.00 billion (average December 2022).
*   Top three sources of DAU growth in December 2023 were India, Bangladesh, and Nigeria.

**Monthly Active Users (MAUs):**
*   **Definition:** A registered and logged-in Facebook user who visited Facebook or used Messenger in the last 30 days.
*   As of December 31, 2023, worldwide MAUs were 3.07 billion, a 3% increase from December 31, 2022.
*   Top three sources of MAU growth in 2023 were India, Bangladesh, and Nigeria.
