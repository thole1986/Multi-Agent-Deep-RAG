**Text Summary:**
*   DAP is defined as a registered and logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp who visited at least one Family product via mobile app or web/mobile browser on a given day.
*   DAP numbers exclude users on other products unless they qualify based on Family product activities.
*   DAP calculations use complex techniques, algorithms, and machine learning models to estimate unique people across multiple accounts/products.
*   Estimates are based on internal reviews and calibrated against user survey data.
*   A September 2022 update contributed approximately 30 million DAP to reported worldwide DAP.
*   Beginning in Q4 2023, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
